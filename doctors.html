ImmunoTracker

Make it easier for your patients to track their immunizations and offer a better expereince as thier healthcare provider

Better Immunization Tracking Saves

It saves you time. No more asking the patient to dig around their past immunization records. Above all, it saves lives. With the outbreak of epidemics becoming more common, your work can be made much easier if the patients you provide healthcare too are tracked for respective immunizations

HIPAA Compliant

The data of your patient will only be accessible to you and your patient. Immunotracker will not share your information or data with any third parties. We consider privacy to be a human right and give it all our attention in our engineering efforts

Verified providers only

Once you sign up we verify your license and inpsect your practice to make sure that you are providing compliant and adequate care to your patients. The ImmunoTracker program will also be incentivised by the government in certain states
