Mission For Doctors

ImmunoTracker

The smartest way to track your child's immunizations. We connect you and your healthcare providers in one platform to make life as a parent a little easier

You probably have a lot on your mind

Meticulously tracking your child's immunization records does not have to be one of those things. Create an account, register your children and add their immunizations to date. Next time you are at the clinic for a vaccine, your doctor will add the immunization to your child's records through ImmunoTracker

Shareable records

Going on a trip aborad or want to provide immunization records to your child's school? Your data is easily available to you. No need to search for those mysterious folders or sift through your documents folder to find immunization records

HIPAA Compliant

Your data will only be accessible to you and your healthcare providers. Immunotracker will not share your information or data with any third parties. We consider privacy to be a human right and give it all our attention in our engineering efforts
